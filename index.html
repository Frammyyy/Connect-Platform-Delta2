Why Use Texas Engineering Interns?

For Students

  • Gain real project experience with Texas employers.
  • Build your resume with relevant, portfolio-ready work.
  • Connect with recruiters looking for emerging talent.
  • Flexible virtual/onsite opportunities across Texas.

For Companies

  • Access skilled student talent from all across Texas.
  • Post real projects matching your team’s needs.
  • Review applications and choose the best fit.
  • Support workforce development in Texas communities.
